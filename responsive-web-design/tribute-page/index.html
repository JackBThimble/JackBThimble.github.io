Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez is a United States Congresswoman representing the 14th district of New York. She is the youngest woman ever to be elected to the House of Representatives and defeated a 10-term incumbent in the 2018 mid-term election by a landslide. She is a very compassionate, determined, and progressive politician often criticized harshly by Republicans and praised immensely by her supporters. She has become prominent figure in the political sphere and continues to fight hard for middle and lower class Americans in the face of ever expanding inequality.
